About Neighboard

Thinking of learning to ride horses? Or maybe you'd like to find a casual guided trail ride nearby, but don't know where to start?

We're on a mission to make horses more accessible to everyone. You'll soon be able to search for the best horseback riding professionals and stables in your area who can help you get started or continue your growth as an equestrian.

Whether you're totally new to horses or looking to tap into your inner horse-whisperer, we'll help you find the best horseback riding lessons or guided trail rides nearby so you can live out your cowboy dreams.

Keep a lookout for our launch in Spring 2024.
